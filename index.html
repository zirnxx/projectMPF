About Us

Welcome to Yesterday Coffee Shop, your cozy corner for the finest coffee experience. Located in Alam Sutera, Yesterday Coffee Shop is more than just a place to get your caffeine fix. It's a community hub where friends meet, ideas flourish, and the aroma of freshly brewed coffee fills the air.

Our journey began with a simple idea: to create a space where people could enjoy high-quality coffee in a warm and inviting atmosphere. We source our beans from the finest coffee-growing regions around the world, ensuring that every cup we serve is rich, flavorful, and full of character. Our skilled baristas are passionate about coffee and take pride in crafting each drink to perfection, from the classic espresso to the creamy latte.

At Yesterday Coffee Shop, we believe that great coffee should be paired with great food. That's why we offer a variety of pastries, sandwiches, and light bites made from the freshest ingredients. Whether you're in the mood for a buttery croissant, a hearty breakfast sandwich, or a slice of our famous cheesecake, you'll find something to satisfy your cravings.

Our commitment to quality extends beyond our coffee and food. We strive to create a welcoming environment where everyone feels at home. Our shop features comfortable seating, free Wi-Fi, and a selection of books and magazines for you to enjoy. We also host regular events, such as live music, poetry readings, and art exhibits, to support local artists and bring the community together.

Sustainability is at the heart of what we do. We use eco-friendly products, support fair trade practices, and work with local suppliers to reduce our environmental footprint. Our goal is to make a positive impact on our community and the planet, one cup of coffee at a time.

Whether you're looking for a place to relax, catch up with friends, or get some work done, Yesterday Coffee Shop is the perfect spot. Come and experience the magic of our coffee shop, where every visit feels like a step back in time to a simpler, more enjoyable moment. We look forward to welcoming you and making your day a little brighter with a great cup of coffee.
